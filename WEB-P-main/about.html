About Our Project

What is Deepfake Video Detection?

Deepfake technology uses artificial intelligence to create highly realistic fake videos by swapping faces or altering facial expressions. Our system detects these manipulations using advanced LSTM (Long Short-Term Memory) networks that analyze temporal patterns in videos.

Why is it Important?

With the rise of deepfake technology, there's an urgent need for reliable detection systems to combat misinformation, protect individuals' identities, and maintain trust in digital media.

How LSTM Improves Detection

LSTM networks excel at analyzing sequential data, making them ideal for detecting inconsistencies in facial movements, blinking patterns, and other temporal features that deepfake algorithms often fail to replicate accurately.
