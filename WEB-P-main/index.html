Understanding DeepFake Technology

DeepFake technology uses artificial intelligence to create highly realistic fake videos by swapping faces or altering facial expressions. While impressive, this technology poses significant risks to information integrity, security, and trust in digital media.

Our LSTM-based detection system analyzes temporal patterns in videos to identify subtle artifacts left by DeepFake generation algorithms, achieving state-of-the-art accuracy in distinguishing real from manipulated content.

How Our Detection Works

LSTM Temporal Analysis

Our system uses Long Short-Term Memory networks to examine frame sequences, detecting inconsistencies in facial movements, blinking patterns, and other temporal features that DeepFakes often fail to replicate accurately.

  • Real-time analysis capabilities
  • Robust against common video compression artifacts
